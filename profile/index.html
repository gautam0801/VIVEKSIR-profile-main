About Us

We offer a wide range of products including LT Switchgears, Motors, PLC, AC & DC Drives, SCADA System, Power & Control Cables, Electrical Measuring Systems, Switches, Power Capacitors. etc....We have a highly qualified and experienced team of engineers with average 15 years experience, state of the art IT infrastructure and dedicated staff to deliver customer delight. Our group company Universal Transformers has been a reliable source for complete Electrical Panel solutions since last 20 years through proven products, innovative technology and exemplary services. At UT we manufacturer of all kind of Electrical Power Distribution / Control Systems having Type Test Certification from CPRI, Bhopal for Short Circuit withstand Test, Temperature Rise Test, HV Test & IP Test among others. The product range extends to PCC , MCC, DG / Turbine / Grid Synchronising Panels, AC / DC Drive Panels, PLC Panels, Air Insulated Bus Ducts Outdoor / Indoor type, Control Transformers, AC Chokes / Reactors. Our manufacturing facilities at Indore and Bhopal are equipped with state-of-the-art modern machinery. With the best of talent, a profound knowledge base and a CAD centre with empowered with latest software and tools, we have been able to position ourselves as a preferred solution provider by our customers.
